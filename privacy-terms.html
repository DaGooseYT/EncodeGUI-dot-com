How your data is managed at EncodeGUI.com:

• Upon registering as a patron on Patreon, we collect the following information: your full name, status of payment (declined, success, etc.), total payment amount (since start of patronage), your Patreon tier, and your email address used to register. This information is copied to a database outside of Patreon to allow services such as the built-in authentication systems in EncodeGUI/FrameGUI to operate properly.

Patreon manages and collects all other payment info and manages it per their terms and conditions. Please DO NOT contact us regarding payment issues as it is simply out of our control and doing so could breach your privacy. Contact Patreon support for anything related to payment issues.



How the authentication system works:

• EncodeGUI/FrameGUI requires an ACTIVE Patreon membership to use, not just to install.

• You will need an internet connection each time you start up EncodeGUI/FrameGUI. After 10 seconds has passed from start-up, EncodeGUI/FrameGUI will not use the internet and you can use it offline thereafter. If no internet connection is present at start-up or there is an error connecting, you will get an error message and then the app will close. Please contact the developer if you are experiencing issues.

• Canceling your Patreon membership or lowering your tier level to "Channel Supporter" will prevent you from using the early access update until you renew, regardless of your accessibility status or payment period in Patreon (this should eventually be changed to where you would still have access until your payment period has ended, but currently there is no way for me to programmatically determine this).

• In order for EncodeGUI/FrameGUI to only require the email address entry once, your email address must be stored on your computer. EncodeGUI/FrameGUI encrypts your email address and stores it locally on your computer. Once you open the app again, EncodeGUI/FrameGUI will decrypt the email address saved from your last successful entry and will send it to the servers on EncodeGUI.com for verification. Any data that is transmitted between EncodeGUI/FrameGUI and the server is always encrypted using SSL.
