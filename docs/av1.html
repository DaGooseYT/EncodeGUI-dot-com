AV1

AV1 consists of the highest quality compression over any other codec in EncodeGUI. EncodeGUI uses the SVT-AV1 encoder which allows automatic hardware acceleration on most modern Intel GPUs.

Recommended for:
High resolution videos (up to 8192x4352), high video compression, ultra-fast encoding.

NOT recommended for:
Wide support; AV1 is a new codec and still doesn't have support on many video players or other platforms.

How to Use

Encode Mode

Constant Rate Factor (1 pass):
This is the recommended option. The video bitrate is automatically adjusted during the encoding process in accordance with the CRF value to provide a high quality output while having a highly compressed video.

Lower values = better quality (min: 0).
Higher value = lower quality (max: 51).
Lossless quality = 0.
Recommend option = 17.

Average Bitrate (1 pass):
Encodes the video in a defined average bitrate (kbits/s). The required bitrate varies on the basis of the resolution of the video, the frame rate, and the colors.

Higher value = higher quality.
Lower value = lower quality.

Colors

The colors option allows you to define the color bit depth for the video.

Bit depth:
Sets the output video color bit depth.
